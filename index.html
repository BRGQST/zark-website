Our Story

Four decades of engineering
meets deep industry knowledge.

Since 1983, we've built and maintained software that financial institutions depend on every day — with the same team, the same values, and an uncompromising focus on accuracy.

Since 1983, Zark Incorporated has been leading the way in custom software solutions for the insurance and finance industries. Our experienced team brings together engineering, business, and actuarial expertise to develop software that is flexible, efficient, and reliable.

At Zark, we understand that smooth operations are crucial when managing critical information — especially in rapidly changing regulatory and market environments. Whether you need a system built from the ground up or a modernized version of your existing platform, we have the depth of experience to get it right.

Our products are trusted by professionals who require accuracy above all else. Every solution we deliver reflects decades of refinement and deep domain knowledge that off-the-shelf software simply cannot replicate.
